About MakeIt

Hello hello!

If you've got the audacity of a baby tiger, but the math skills of an amoeba then I'm sure there's been a time when you picked up a wood related project thinking "it seems easy enough".

Alas; after many hours, and much...much more money spent than what was anticipated; you're left with nothing but regret and horribly cut pieces of wood.

My amazing Cut Calculator takes away the pain of taking measurements to a tee. With this calculator, you can remain confident knowing it wasn't your mathematical skills holding you back.

So go ahead now and look at some new projects to pick up or try the cut calculator for your current project.
